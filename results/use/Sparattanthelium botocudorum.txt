General InformationSparattanthelium botocudorum is a semideciduous shrub or small tree with a dense, irregular crown; it can grow 3 - 5 metres tall. The stems are somewhat scandent, giving the impression of a climbing plant. The short, crooked bole can be 10 - 20cm in diameter[625TitleBrazilian Trees. Volume 3.
